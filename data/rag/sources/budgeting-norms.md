# Budgeting Norms by Region for LGBTQ+ Travel

Planning your budget carefully allows you to maximize your experience while keeping costs under control.

## Europe and North America
In top destinations like Mykonos, Ibiza, Palm Springs, and Provincetown, costs can be quite high, especially during peak summer months or major events like Pride and circuit festivals. Expect premium pricing for accommodation and nightlife. To save money, consider traveling during the shoulder season (spring or early autumn) when the weather is still pleasant but prices are lower.

## Latin America and Southeast Asia
Destinations such as Puerto Vallarta, Mexico City, Bangkok, and Phuket often offer more budget-friendly options. Your money can go further here regarding dining, accommodation, and nightlife. Gay-owned guesthouses are a fantastic option across these regions—they often provide excellent value and serve as great social hubs for meeting other travelers.

## Daily Spending Strategy
Regardless of the destination, it is wise to separate your daily spending budget into categories: lodging, dining, nightlife, and excursions. This ensures that an unexpected splurge on a big party night or a special dinner does not derail your budget for the rest of the trip.
