# Health and Travel Tips for LGBTQ+ Travelers

Staying healthy and prepared while traveling ensures you can fully enjoy your trip without unnecessary interruptions.

## Medication and Prescriptions
Always carry enough medication (such as PrEP, ARVs, or hormones) for your entire trip, plus a few days' extra. Keep medications in their original, clearly labeled prescription bottles in your carry-on luggage. Some countries have restrictions on importing certain medications, even for personal use, so verify with the destination's embassy or official health resources before you fly.

## Vaccinations and Clinics
Familiarize yourself with local LGBTQ+-friendly health clinics in your destination. In major gay travel hubs, you can often find clinics that cater specifically to the community. Additionally, check if any specific vaccinations (like Hepatitis A and B, or mpox) are recommended for your travel activities or destination.
